🌐 GLOBEVAULT – UNLOCK THE WORLD

GlobeVault is the ultimate geography and culture quiz game. Journey across every continent, unlock 100 nations, and test your knowledge through 500 handcrafted challenges.

─── HOW IT WORKS ───

🗺️ EXPLORE BY REGION
Browse 5 world regions — Africa, Americas, Asia, Europe, and Oceania & the Middle East. Unlock nations one by one as you prove your knowledge.

🧠 500 UNIQUE CHALLENGES
Every nation has 5 challenges mixing multiple-choice questions and type-in answers. Questions cover capitals, currencies, history, culture, landmarks, and more.

⭐ EARN STARS, TRACK PROGRESS
Score 5/5 for 3 stars. Beat 3/5 for 2. Every nation keeps your personal best, and region progress bars show how close you are to mastering each area.

🔊 IMMERSIVE AMBIENT AUDIO
Each world region has its own ambient music score, creating an atmospheric backdrop as you explore the globe.

🔒 UNLOCK AS YOU LEARN
Nations unlock progressively — complete a country to open the next. Knowledge earned, not bought.
